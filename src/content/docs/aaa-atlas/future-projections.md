## What this page covers

What's on the horizon for African climate-projection science: CMIP7, the next IPCC assessment cycle (AR7), and the CORDEX-CMIP6 dynamical-downscaling build-out. What each will (and won't) change for the Atlas.

## CMIP7

*To be written. Sources: WCRP-CMIP CMIP7 Fast Track page; ScenarioMIP for CMIP7 (van Vuuren et al. GMD 2026). Cover:*

- *Timeline: simulations 2026; first results 2026-2027; AR7 working-group deliverables ~late 2026 onwards*
